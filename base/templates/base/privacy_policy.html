Your Rights

 You have rights under privacy laws to control your personal information. We respect and support these rights in all jurisdictions where we operate. These include:
  • Right to know whether or not we process your personal data.
  • Right to access and receive a copy of your personal data.
  • Right to correct inaccurate or outdated information.
  • Right to delete your personal data, unless retention is required by law.
  • Right to portability — obtain a copy of your data in a transferable format.
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights.
  • Right to opt out of processing for marketing or profiling. We do not use data for such purposes.
We do not conduct automated decision-making or profiling using OAuth data. All processing is based on explicit user actions or consent.

How to Exercise Your Rights

 To exercise your rights, you can contact us by submitting a data subject access request or by emailing us at contact@internstart.com. We may verify your identity before processing any request, as required by law.

You may also authorize an agent to submit requests on your behalf, provided they have written permission and comply with verification requirements. We will never discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.
An authorized agent may make a request on your behalf if they provide valid written permission and proof of identity. We may deny a request from an authorized agent that fails to provide documentation verifying their authority as required by applicable privacy laws.

Request Verification

 When we receive a request, we must verify your identity to ensure we are acting on behalf of the correct individual. We only use the information provided in your request to confirm your identity or authority. If we cannot verify your identity using existing data, we may request additional information for verification and security purposes. This information will not be used for any other reason.

If your request is submitted through an authorized agent, we may ask for a signed authorization from you confirming that they are permitted to act on your behalf before processing the request.

Appeals

 If we deny your request, you may appeal our decision by emailing contact@internstart.com. We will respond in writing, explaining our reasoning and actions taken. If your appeal is denied, you may contact your state’s Attorney General or local data protection authority.

 California residents may request, once per year and free of charge, information regarding any personal data (if applicable) disclosed to third parties for their own direct marketing purposes during the prior calendar year.
